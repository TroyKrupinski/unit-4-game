Crystals Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals, clicking on one will give you a specific value towards your score.

You win the game by matching your total score to random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each crystals is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of the crystals
